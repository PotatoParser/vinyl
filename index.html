Selecting between Video & Audio

To select Video, click on the icon.

To select Audio, click on the icon.

Video can be canceled by hovering over the queued items and clicking.

Advanced Settings

The following requires you to open the Advanced Settings by clicking on it.

Options marked with are experimental and take up processing power.

Video Quality: The video with the highest quality matching the selected one, will be downloaded.

Audio Quality (kbps): The audio with the highest bitrate matching the selected one, will be downloaded.

Video Format: Downloads the video available in the selected format.

Audio Format: Downloads the audio available in the selected format (Excludes mp3).

Convert (Experimental): When enabled, downloads closest matching video/audio to selected qualities and converts them to the selected video/audio formats.
